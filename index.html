About Me

My Background

I am 28 years old and I am originally from Columbus, Ohio. In the U.S. I earned my Bachelor's in Criminal Justice and my Master's in Criminology. During my education, I worked various jobs relating to my field including a year as a private police officer working in residential areas. In 2013, my life quickly changed after I met my wife, Mirjam. My wife is originally from Sweden, and after I became engaged to her I moved to Gothenburg. Since arriving in Sweden, I have studied Swedish up to the SAS-Grund level. I also worked for a year as an English teacher with preschool and kindergarten students who came from English speaking families.

Change in Career

Finding work in Criminology has not been easy in Sweden for various reasons. As a result, I have decided to switch career paths. However, I am very excited for this change in direction. Computers, networking, and to a certain extent programming have always been passions of mine, but I did not pursue a career in the IT field due to job insecurity in that area at the time. Since I am able to receive free education in Sweden, I plan to make the most of it by reeducating myself for a career as a front-end web developer.

Career Goals

Currently I am studying Front-End JavaScript Development at Lernia. In this program, we will be learning HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Angular, Express, Node and other related technologies. After finishing my education with Lernia, I hope to find work as a front-end developer but I also have great interest in eventually working with back-end JavaScript programming. With time, I hope to transition into working in more of a full stack development capacity. I am extremely interested in both Linux and the open source community as whole. I think it would be very exciting to eventually learn Linux server administration to further my ability to work with back-end web technologies.
